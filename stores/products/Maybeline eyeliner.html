Tatoo Eyeliner

Benefits

Meet our 1st blendable mechanical gel pencil liner. Just blend and set for a smoldering smokey look. Maybelline NY’s Tattoo Studio Smokey Gel Pencil glides on smooth for a sultry blend and smoldering tattoo matte finish. This smudge proof eyeliner doesn’t smear—even in the inner line. It’s a long lasting waterproof eyeliner that’s formulated with powder in a gel, leaving behind a smokey, blurred and elegant look. Plus, it’s designed with a precision application tip for ultimate control. Now available in three shades, Smokey Black, Smokey Grey, and Smokey Brown. Ophthalmologist tested and is suitable for contact lens wearers.

How to use:

Step 1: Apply Tattoo Studio Smokey Gel Pencil Eyeliner along the upper lid and lower lash line for intensity. Step 2: Use the blender to smudge out the look. Sets for up to 36HR. Step 3: Eyeliner is easily removed at home with Maybelline Expert Eyes 100% Oil-Free Makeup Remover
