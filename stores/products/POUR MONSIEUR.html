Product Details

Description

Masculine distinction according to Gabrielle Chanel. A discreet and yet surprising woody-chypre. A refined and timeless composition.

Composition

A sober yet intense woody-chypre fragrance.

A fresh, citrusy burst that reveals a deep heart with the spicy notes of nutmeg and a woody trail enhanced with a touch of Vanilla from Madagascar.

Inspiration

.POUR MONSIEUR eau de parfum reinterprets the first men's fragrance launched by Gabrielle Chanel in 1955. A more intense version of the original fragrance that still retains its sober character. For an elegant, charismatic, quick-witted man.

Art of perfuming

The eau de parfum is sprayed directly on skin or inside clothing for a more intense scent. It is defined by a more fragrant composition with a higher concentration of noble raw materials for a more pronounced character. A full range of shaving and body products is available for an intensified scent throughout the day.
